Timed games have arrived.
Play with a clock. Blitz or Normal — your call.

You asked for a way to keep games moving. Now every table can run on a chess-style clock: a time bank for each player, with a few seconds added back on every turn you take. Let it run low and you get flagged — and your opponent can claim the win.

Prefer the classic pace? Leave a table Untimed and nothing changes.

Blitz
3 minutes + 15 seconds a turn. Fast, tense, decisive.
Normal
10 minutes + 30 seconds a turn. Room to think.
Claim victory
When an opponent flags, the claim button appears the instant it happens — no reload, no waiting.
